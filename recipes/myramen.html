Ingredients

  • Maru-chan chicken-flavored ramen
  • Sesame oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water (per serving of ramen used) to a boil in a pot
  2. Add egg(s) and stir to prevent sticking to bottom
  3. When egg is almost cooked, add ramen noodles
  4. After noodles separate, add seasoning packet and sesame oil until you can smell it
  5. Cook until noodles are soft, serve HOT!
