Pork Adobo

Description

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added. Adobo is a popular dish in the Philippines, along with Sinigang. Adobo, in general, can be cooked using different kinds of protein.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Pork cut of choice (my recommendation is pork belly)
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Bay leaf
  • Water

Steps

  1. Measure 8oz of water into a measuring cup
  2. To the same cup, add 2oz vinegar and 5oz soy sauce
  3. Add 1 tbsp garlic powder
  4. Add 1tsp pepper, 1 tsp salt
  5. Chop pork into bite-sized pieces
  6. Add to pot
